Glucose serum test result : indique la valeur du résultat ou si le test n’a pas été réalisé | Values: “>200,” “>300,” “normal,” and “none” if not measured
A1c test result : indique la valeur du résultat ou si le test n’a pas été réalisé | Values: “>8” if the result was greater than 8%, 
											    “>7” if the result was greater than 7% but less than 8%, 
											    “normal” if the result was less than 7%, and “none” if not measured.

Change of medications  : indique si il y a eu un changement de medicament pour le diabète | Values: “change” and “no change”
Diabetes medications : indique si il y a eu prescription de médicaments pour le diabète | Values: “yes” and “no"



24 features for medications : Ces  features indiquent si le médicament a été prescript ou non ou si il y a eu un changement de dosage 
Values: “up” if the dosage was increased during the encounter,“down” if the dosage was decreased, “steady” if the dosage did not change, and “no” if the drug was not prescribed


Readmitted : nombre de jours écoulés avant la réadmission d’un patient 

Values: “<30” if the patient was readmitted in less than 30 days, “>30” if the patient was readmitted in more than 30 days, and “No” for no record of readmission.
